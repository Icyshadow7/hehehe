About Me

I am a student web designer from Kathmandu, Nepal, passionate about crafting stylish, user-friendly, functional, and efficient websites. It all began with a simple question — “How do websites actually work?” That one question opened up an entire world of information to me. What started as curiosity soon transformed into a deep passion. I found joy in creating interfaces, solving layout puzzles, and breathing life into static designs through code. Driven by a relentless thirst for knowledge, I dove deep into the vast realm of web technologies, mastering HTML, CSS, and Python — a journey that naturally led me to explore modern frameworks and design tools. Every new project has become a fresh opportunity to grow, experiment, and refine my craft. My greatest passion lies in transforming ideas into interactive, responsive, and dynamic web experiences. Whether it's a personal portfolio, a business website, or a full-fledged web application, I focus on every detail to ensure the final product is not only visually captivating but also highly functional. In the ever-evolving world of the web, the constant emergence of new tools and techniques keeps me inspired. The challenges, the learning, and the thrill of discovery — this is what drives me every day.
